--- layout: post title: 'archlinux mirrors otimizados ' date: '2014-03-15T12:04:00.001-03:00' author: Murilo Pontes tags: - otimizado - ArchLinux - mirrors - optimized modified_time: '2015-01-18T11:42:44.796-03:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-3047384083282566923.post-2512504508064143323 blogger_orig_url: http://dronespersonalizados.blogspot.com/2014/03/archlinux-mirrors-otimizados.html --- Instalar os pacotes reflector e rsync
pacman -S reflector rsync



Atualizar a lista de mirrors

[root@localhost ~]# reflector -f 10 --verbose --save /etc/pacman.d/mirrorlist

O resultado final:

[root@localhost ~]# cat /etc/pacman.d/mirrorlist
